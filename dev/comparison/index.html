Comparison Results

The results of using PowerModelsADAv0.1 on 9 selected test cases with three distributed algorithms and 5 power flow formulations are shown here. We run the three distributed algorithms on a high-performance computing service with a 16-core CPU and 16GB of RAM. The results here used PowerModelsADA v0.1 in Julia v1.6. The results use Ipopt solver for the polar and rectangular ACOPF and Gurobi for the DC approximation, and SOCP and QC relaxations of the OPF.
